    • About

    From an early age, I acquired skills in photoshop and digital drawing, standing out for my constant learning. Graduated as an Illustrator and Animator at the Escola d'Art i Disseny de Sant Cugat, I consolidated my skills and artistic bases, fulfilling a goal by projecting a short film at the animation festival of Lleida (ANIMAC).

    With experience as a graphic designer I expanded my marketing startup experience, performing various functions ranging from social media design to web layout, UI and prepress. During the COVID-19 crisis, I decided to evolve professionally and enrolled in the Master of Concept Art and Character Design at the Escola de Cinema de Barcelona, where I had the privilege to improve my skills and career path. In 2023 I had the opportunity to join the Scruffy Dog team as a Concept Artist, a fact that boosted my professional career, improving my English level and assuming more prominent roles as a tutor for trainees.

    I always give my all in every project, looking for solutions inside and outside my area of work and collaborating as a team so that the final result is the best possible.
